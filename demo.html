🔒 Privacy is the architecture, not a policy

Built to make the answer genuinely private — and what we'd ask of Google

QuestionsAnswer is a privacy-first AI search experience built on Google Gemini. Conversations are never used to train AI, never sold, and nothing about them is retained on our servers. We want to take that one step further with Google's help. Here is exactly what is already true today, and the one capability we'd ask Google to provide.

🏢Never trained on your data

Built on Google Gemini Enterprise (Vertex AI). Under Google Cloud's enterprise terms, prompts and answers are never used to train models and aren't read by humans.

🗑️Nothing stored on our servers

Your chat history and any documents you add live encrypted in your own browser. We keep no logs, no profile, no transcript tied to you.

🔐Encrypted in transit, on-device context

Every question travels over encrypted HTTPS straight to Google. Add your own files and they're indexed on your device for better answers — never uploaded.

What we can build today

  • Chat history and uploaded documents stored encrypted in the browser — they never leave the device
  • On-device RAG: your files give the AI better context without ever being uploaded
  • Zero-retention relay — the server stores nothing and logs nothing
  • A path where the question never passes through our servers at all (Firebase AI Logic + App Check, calling Gemini through Google's own proxy)
  • The no-training guarantee via the Vertex AI enterprise tier

What we'd ask Google to provide

  • Developer access to confidential inference (Private AI Compute), so each query is processed only inside a sealed hardware enclave — invisible even to Google's own operators
  • A client-verifiable attestation token, so the user's browser can cryptographically confirm the sealed environment before sending anything
  • A contractual no-access, enclave-processed, zero-retention guarantee for this tier
Demo status: this is a working demo. It currently runs on Google's free Gemini API so we can show the full experience end to end. Once Google reviews and approves the project, we intend to switch to a proper Gemini / Vertex AI enterprise tier — making the no-training and confidential-processing guarantees above contractually and technically real for every user.
